BNG Elite Circle

Our loyalty program is designed to offer you prestige, comfort, and a personalized experience with every ride. Earn points, enjoy tiered benefits, and redeem exclusive rewards.

Tiers of Loyalty

Silver Tier

Qualification: Book 5 rides within 6 months

Benefits: 5% off each ride, priority booking, access to special promotions

Gold Tier

Qualification: Book 15 rides within 12 months

Benefits: 10% off, priority booking, complimentary upgrades to better vehicles (subject to availability)

Platinum Tier

Qualification: Book 30 rides within 12 months

Benefits: 15% off, guaranteed vehicle upgrades, personalized greeting, priority booking with guaranteed availability

Earn and Redeem BNG Prestige Points

Earn points for every ride and redeem them for exclusive rewards:

  • 1 point per $1 spent on each ride
  • Additional points for booking during off-peak times or referring new customers
  • Redeeming Points: $1 for every 50 points
  • Points expire every 12 months

Referral Program

Earn bonus points by referring friends or family. When your referred friend makes their first booking, both of you will receive bonus points!
